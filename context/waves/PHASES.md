# MUNDUS Phases

MUNDUS uses wave records to keep catalog curation, rights posture, and promotion
paths explicit.

## Phase 1 - Foundation

Establish the repo, product thesis, registry conventions, seed catalog, and
FLETCH validation/search contract.

## Phase 2 - Catalog Expansion

Add source families and known assets across open education, standards, public
science, government reference systems, and public-domain/classic corpora.

## Phase 3 - Promotion Bridge

Define how MUNDUS rows become FONTES source-custody records, PROOF ledger rows,
PEBBLE packs, and CROP views.

## Phase 4 - Review and Governance

Add review roles, rights posture audits, stale-link reports, deprecation policy,
and source-family curation gates.

## Phase 5 - Search and Publishing

Publish derived registry indexes, catalog slices, and local status reports for
agents and downstream repos.
