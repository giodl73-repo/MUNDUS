# MUNDUS Research

Use this skill before adopting a new source family, rights posture, or catalog
taxonomy.

## Research checklist

- Verify canonical owner and URL.
- Record likely rights posture and license URL when available.
- Decide whether MUNDUS should catalog only or recommend FONTES promotion.
- Identify stable tags, metadata keys, and downstream consumers.
- Cite sources in the research summary before changing registries.

## Output

Return candidate rows with URL, owner, domain, rights posture, suggested
`fetch_policy`, tags, and promotion route.
